Lord Hanuman is a revered deity in Hinduism, celebrated as the ultimate symbol of devotion, strength, and selfless service. He is the son of Kesari and Anjana, and is also known as the spiritual son of Vayu, the wind god. Born with divine powers, he possesses immense physical strength and the ability to assume any form at will. His unwavering devotion to Lord Rama is the central theme of his legendary tales in the Hindu epic, the Ramayana. Hanuman played a crucial role in the epic by leaping across the ocean to Lanka to discover Goddess Sita's whereabouts. He is also famous for carrying an entire mountain containing the Sanjeevani herb to save the life of Lakshmana.
